Here is the first implementation of the character creator/builder for pf2e. At the moment it is just a simple builder that works for Setting up a character, primarily the ABCs of character creation.

Currently Implemented

  • Ancestry Selection
  • Heritage Selection
  • Background Selection
  • Class Selection
  • Level elements provided
  • Feat/Feature selection per level up
  • Ability score selection

Not Yet Implemented

  • Spellcasting help/management
  • Skill training management
  • Sources selection/toggle
  • Character naming/level up directly from builder
  • Token and Portrait Management
  • Deity Selection
